Battery Mod Instructions:

Nedded:

-2 10KOhm resistors
-Soldering Iron
-Solder


the idea is to make a Voltaje divider between positive lead of battery,
A2 pin of arduino and arduino GND.


Positive lead----10K resistor----A2----10K resistor----GND
|            |                 |                          |
   battery                              Arduino
